Built for teams

Two roles, one system

Role 01
Manager
  • Take and clean meeting notes with AI
  • Assign action items to team members
  • View all members' progress in one dashboard
  • Track meeting health scores over time
  • Get alerted to meeting debt and overloaded owners
  • Export meeting summaries to PDF or Markdown
Role 02
Team Member
  • Log in to see only your assigned tasks
  • Mark tasks as Todo, In Progress or Done
  • See priority level for each action item
  • Get notified when new tasks are assigned
  • View your personal completion rate
  • Flag blockers back to the manager
